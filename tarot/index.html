WHAT IS AN ASTROTAROT READING?

For three decades I have worked with the tarot. The deep and significant symbols have become second nature to me and I “speak the language” fluently.

Tarot is an art of symbols and intuition. Many people claim to “read cards” but it is more than understanding the general meaning assigned to particular cards and more the gift of the reader to use these symbols to tune into the higher wisdom and information.

The universal symbols of the tarot transcend culture, language and time. The tarot can show basic symbolic and collective experiences. The reader, if gifted, can use these symbols to tap into their psychic higher self and gain key information for the client. Doctor Carl Jung, the famous psychologist, studied and used tarot. He related tarot to his theory of the Collective Unconscious.

My intuitive gifts are well developed through my years of study, working with tarot cards, hundreds of clients and the gift being passed down to me through my family lineage.
